About Our Resort & Spa

Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Mahabaleshwar, Strawberry King Resort & Spa offers a unique blend of luxury and relaxation. Our resort is renowned for its strawberry-themed experiences and world-class spa treatments.

Each room is designed for comfort with modern amenities, private balconies, and stunning views of the hills and valleys. Enjoy fresh strawberry delicacies, spa sessions, and proximity to Mahabaleshwar's attractions.

Whether you're seeking a rejuvenating spa retreat or an adventurous getaway, Strawberry King is the perfect destination for couples, families, and wellness enthusiasts.
